Privacy Policy for Instagram DM Bot

Last updated: January 2025

Information We Collect

Our Instagram DM automation bot collects and processes:

  • Instagram account information (username, account type) when you authenticate
  • Comments on your Instagram posts that contain specified keywords
  • Basic profile information of users who comment on monitored posts

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Monitor your Instagram posts for relevant comments
  • Send automated direct messages to users who comment with specific keywords
  • Maintain logs of sent messages to prevent duplicates

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely and used only for the automated messaging functionality. We do not share your information with third parties.

Instagram API Usage

This application uses Instagram's official Business API in compliance with Instagram's Platform Policy and Terms of Use.

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary to provide the service. You can revoke access at any time through your Instagram account settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Revoke authorization at any time

Contact Information

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page.
